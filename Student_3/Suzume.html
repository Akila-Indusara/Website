Suzume no Tojimari


     Suzume no Tojimari (translated as "Suzume's Door-Closing Journey") is a 2022 Japanese animated adventure film written and directed by Makoto Shinkai. The film stars Nanoka Hara as Suzume, a 17-year-old girl who embarks on a journey to close a series of doors that are releasing disasters all over Japan.
  • English Title - Suzume
  • Japanese Title - すずめの戸締まり (Suzume no Tojimari)
  • Producers - STORY
  • Adaptation - Suzume no Tojimari manga by Makoto Shinkai(Story), Denki Amashima (Art)
  • Studios - CoMix Wave Films

     The film is set in a post-apocalyptic Japan, where a series of natural disasters have caused widespread destruction. Suzume, a 17-year-old girl from Kyushu, is on her way to school when she meets a young man named Souta. Souta is searching for an abandoned area with a door, and Suzume directs him to an abandoned onsen resort nearby. Suzume follows Souta to the resort, and there she discovers a door standing alone on its frame. When she opens the door, she witnesses a starlit field inside, which she can't enter.
     Suzume soon realizes that the door she has opened is one of many doors that have been appearing all over Japan. These doors are not ordinary doors; they are portals to other worlds, and they are releasing all sorts of disasters into the real world. Suzume is tasked with finding and closing these doors before it's too late.
     Along the way, Suzume meets a variety of people who help her on her journey. She also learns more about the doors and their origins. The film explores themes of closure, healing, and the power of the human spirit.
     The film's visuals are stunning. Makoto Shinkai is a master of animation, and he uses his skills to create a visually stunning film that is both beautiful and atmospheric. The film's use of light and shadow is particularly effective, and the film's landscapes are breathtaking.
     The film's story is emotionally resonant. Suzume's journey is one of closure, healing, and the power of the human spirit. The film explores these themes in a way that is both meaningful and moving. The film also features a strong female protagonist, which is refreshing to see in a genre that is often dominated by male characters.
     The film's message is hopeful. Suzume no Tojimari is a film about the power of hope in the face of adversity. The film shows that even in the darkest of times, there is always hope for a better future.
