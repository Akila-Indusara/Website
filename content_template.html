Main Cast


Elijah Wood

Frodo Baggins
     Elijah Wood is a talented American actor known for his diverse and captivating performances. Born on January 28, 1981, in Cedar Rapids, Iowa, Wood rose to prominence for his portrayal of Frodo Baggins in "The Lord of the Rings" trilogy. His portrayal of the determined and humble hobbit captivated audiences worldwide. Beyond Middle-earth, Wood has demonstrated his versatility in various film genres, including dramas, thrillers, and comedies. With his distinctive blue eyes and natural charisma, Wood has established himself as a respected and accomplished actor, leaving an indelible mark on the film industry.

Viggo Mortensen

Aragorn
     Viggo Mortensen is a highly acclaimed Danish-American actor, poet, musician, and photographer. Born on October 20, 1958, in New York, Mortensen has gained recognition for his versatile performances and dedication to his craft. He is best known for his role as Aragorn in "The Lord of the Rings" trilogy, where he brought the noble and charismatic character to life. Mortensen's intense and committed performances have earned him critical acclaim and numerous accolades throughout his career. With his striking presence, depth of emotion, and artistic versatility, Mortensen has become a respected and influential figure in the world of cinema.

Sean Astin

Samwise Gamgee
     Sean Astin is a talented American actor, director, and producer. Born on February 25, 1971, in Santa Monica, California, Astin has been involved in a wide range of film and television projects. He is best known for his role as Samwise Gamgee in "The Lord of the Rings" trilogy, where he portrayed the loyal and courageous hobbit. Astin's heartfelt and endearing performance resonated with audiences, earning him praise and recognition. With his versatile acting skills, dedication to his craft, and involvement in various creative endeavors, Astin has established himself as a respected and beloved figure in the entertainment industry.

Dominic Monaghand

Meriadoc Brandybuck
     Dominic Monaghan is a talented English actor and wildlife enthusiast. Born on December 8, 1976, in Berlin, Germany, Monaghan rose to fame for his portrayal of Meriadoc Brandybuck, or Merry, in "The Lord of the Rings" trilogy. His charismatic and endearing performance as the adventurous hobbit won the hearts of audiences worldwide. Monaghan's versatility extends beyond Middle-earth, as he has taken on various roles in film and television, including the hit series "Lost." In addition to his acting career, Monaghan is known for his passion for wildlife and has hosted nature documentaries, showcasing his dedication to environmental conservation.

Orlando Bloom

Legolas
     Orlando Bloom is a talented English actor who gained international recognition for his role as Legolas in "The Lord of the Rings" trilogy. Born on January 13, 1977, in Canterbury, England, Bloom's portrayal of the skilled elven archer captivated audiences with his grace and agility. Beyond Middle-earth, Bloom has appeared in various films, including the "Pirates of the Caribbean" series and "The Hobbit" trilogy. Known for his handsome looks and charismatic presence, Bloom has established himself as a versatile actor, showcasing his talent in both action-packed blockbusters and intimate dramas, leaving a lasting impact on the world of cinema.
